# Bestiole Tuning Sheet

Targets
- Daily care: 1-3 quick actions
- No punishment for absence
- Utility-focused support

Suggested Values
Needs decay per day
- Hunger: -5
- Energy: -4
- Mood: -6
- Cleanliness: -5
- Bond: -1 (only after 3 days no care)

Care gains
- Feed: +20 Hunger, +4 Mood
- Play: +15 Mood, -4 Energy
- Groom: +18 Cleanliness, +3 Bond
- Rest: +20 Energy
- Gift: +8 Bond (1/day)

Combat scaling
- Bond 0-3: basic actions only
- Bond 4-7: unlock 1 form action
- Bond 8-12: unlock 2 form actions

Cooldowns
- Guard: 1 turn
- Ward: 2 turns
- Spur: 2 turns
- Veil Step: 3 turns

Event influence
- Support modifier = (Mood + Bond) * 0.5
- Cap at +5 total

Balancing Knobs
- Increase care gains by 10% if playtests feel too slow
- Reduce night bonuses if Bestiole feels mandatory

QA
- Bestiole should never be required to win.
- Missing 7 days should still be recoverable in 1-2 sessions.
