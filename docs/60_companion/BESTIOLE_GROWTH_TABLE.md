# Bestiole Growth Table

Use this as a content balance guide (no code).

Needs (decay per day)
- Hunger: -5
- Energy: -4
- Mood: -6
- Cleanliness: -5
- Bond: -1 (only if no care in 3 days)

Care Actions (typical gains)
- Feed: +20 Hunger, +4 Mood
- Play: +15 Mood, -4 Energy
- Groom: +18 Cleanliness, +3 Bond
- Rest: +20 Energy
- Gift (1/day): +8 Bond

Training (weekly)
- Trail Run: +1 Agility, +1 Focus
- Oath Practice: +1 Spirit, +1 Bond
- Stone Carry: +1 Vitality, +1 Focus
- Calm Rite: +1 Mood, +1 Spirit

Growth Milestones
- Pup -> Adept: Bond >= 6 and 5 care days completed
- Adept -> Elder: Bond >= 12 and 2 seasonal milestones met

Seasonal Modifiers
- Winter: Energy decay -1 (slower), Bond gain -1 (slower)
- Spring: Mood gains +2
- Summer: Training gain +1
- Autumn: Spirit influence +1 in events

Catch-Up
- If absent 3+ days: Bond decay stops
- First care after absence: +5 Bond bonus

Notes
- Adjust values for playtest pacing.
- Keep actions under 20 seconds.
