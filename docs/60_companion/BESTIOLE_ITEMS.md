# Bestiole Items (Care + Utility)

Items are simple, low-grind, and optional.

Care Items
- Soft Feed: +Hunger, small Mood
- Warm Broth: +Energy, small Cleanliness
- Herbal Pack: +Mood, small Bond
- Clean Wrap: +Cleanliness, small Mood

Utility Items
- Small Bell: improves Scout action chance
- Oath Charm: reduces promise penalty once per cycle
- Fog Pendant: increases night event influence
- Trail Ribbon: improves path discovery

Treats (limited)
- Rare Treat: temporary boost to Bond for 1 day
- Calm Treat: temporary conflict reduction

Notes
- Items should be easy to obtain.
- Treats are limited to avoid stacking.
