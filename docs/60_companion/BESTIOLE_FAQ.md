# Bestiole FAQ (Player Facing)

Q: Do I need to care for Bestiole every day?
A: No. Missing days never blocks progress. Care just adds soft bonuses.

Q: Can Bestiole be harmed?
A: No. It does not die. It only changes mood and bond.

Q: Does Bestiole make me stronger?
A: It gives support actions and utility, not raw power spikes.

Q: Can I change its form?
A: Yes, once per season, without penalty.

Q: What happens if I break a promise?
A: Bestiole reacts with mood loss and fog response, but recovery is quick.

Q: Is there a best form?
A: No. Each form favors different play styles.
