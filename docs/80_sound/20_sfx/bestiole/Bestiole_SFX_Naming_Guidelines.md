# Bestiole SFX Naming + 10 Sample IDs

Naming rules
- Prefix: bestiole_
- Family: chirp | purr | trill | huff | whimper | pop
- Descriptor: soft | bright | low | play | tired
- Variant index: _01.._05

Format
bestiole_<family>_<descriptor>_<index>

Example IDs (10)
- bestiole_chirp_soft_01
- bestiole_chirp_soft_02
- bestiole_trill_bright_01
- bestiole_trill_bright_02
- bestiole_purr_soft_01
- bestiole_purr_soft_02
- bestiole_huff_low_01
- bestiole_huff_low_02
- bestiole_whimper_soft_01
- bestiole_pop_play_01

Asset rules
- Mono WAV, 16-bit, 22050 or 44100 Hz
- Duration: 80-350 ms (except loops)
- No dialogue content, no phoneme shapes
- Keep consistent loudness across families
