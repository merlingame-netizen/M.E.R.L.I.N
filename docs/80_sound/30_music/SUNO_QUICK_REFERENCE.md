## Critical Rules

1. **Always enable Instrumental Mode** in SUNO
2. **Use v5** for production (better instrument separation)
3. **Generate 6+ takes** per prompt
4. **Add "seamless loop"** for gameplay tracks
5. **BPM must match** Tempo Map (see MERLIN_MUSIC_TEMPO_MAP.md)
6. **No electronic sounds** except for retro variants
7. **Max 6 instruments** per prompt
