# Known Risks and Mitigations (World Rules)

This document lists risks for a permanent world design and mitigation ideas.

Risks
1) FOMO pressure from dated events
- Mitigation: hours only boost, not block; repeat seasonal arcs; offer recap events

2) Timezone unfairness
- Mitigation: use local time; allow 2-3 hour grace window; show next 7 days

3) Promise harshness causes churn
- Mitigation: first debt per cycle softened; ritual release options; clear debt quests

4) Seasonal exclusivity locks content
- Mitigation: provide short "echo" events after season ends; archive via lore books

5) Night dominance over day content
- Mitigation: keep day craft/resource bonuses strong; add daytime exclusive events

6) Conflict spirals block casual players
- Mitigation: allow conflict drop actions; cap conflict delta per day

7) Event overlap stacking too strong
- Mitigation: cap combined multiplier; prioritize only top 2 bonuses

8) Progression too fast for long-term goals
- Mitigation: utility unlocks are fast; full collections require seasons

9) Too many achievements at once
- Mitigation: stagger unlocks; group by category; limit concurrent popups

10) Lying to Merlin feels unfair
- Mitigation: communicate near-impossible odds; provide safer alternatives

QA Focus
- Verify no content is fully locked behind time windows
- Verify debt removal is always possible within a cycle
- Verify at least one major event per season during daytime
