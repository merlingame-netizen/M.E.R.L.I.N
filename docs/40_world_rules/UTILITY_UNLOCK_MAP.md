# Utility Unlock Map

This document maps achievements to utilitarian rewards.

Unlocks (Tools/Perks)
- Lanterne d Alignement: reveal lies and spirit traces
- Registre des Serments: promise list and debt timer
- Calendrier des Brumes: next 7 events with bonuses
- Aiguille des Tensions: conflict level indicator
- Sac a Runes: inventory utility
- Boussole des Menhirs: navigation aid
- Filet des Korrigans: capture trick clues
- Cle de Brume: open fog doors
- Coupe Ronce: clear blocked paths
- Journal des Veilles: log event bonuses
- Appat a Feux Follets: summon a spirit encounter
- Pochette de Semis: morning harvest boost
- Marqueur de Sentier: mark hidden path
- Sablier des Heures: show next hourly bonuses
- Berceau de Bestiole: stabilize taniere gauges after absence
- Codex de Runes: show rune sources and synergies
- Accordeur d Esprit: choose a spirit target weekly
- Carnet des Pistes: list discovered hidden paths
- Ancrage du Lien: Bestiole bond decays slower
- Echo de Brume: replay a key clue after a mystery
- Rythme Stable: daily streak grace once
- Bourse d Echange: extra trade per day
- Lentille des Marees: show best time phase for action
- Resonance Saisonniere: small season bonus when present in a row
- Compas d Omen: detect a nearby anomaly
